Instruction

The "By Year" option enables selection of a specific

year among 5 available yea for displaying the epidemic

morbidity of all provinces in China. To explore data of

a specific province, please click on the location of that

province on the map. Meanwhile, the "By Province" option

enables direct selection of the province of interest, and

a marker will be placed on the selected province, together

with the morbidity information of all years for that province.

Due to data accessibility, the data of Hongkong, Macau, and<

Taiwan will not be shown on the map.
